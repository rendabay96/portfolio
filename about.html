Brenda Hu is a UX Designer based in New York

About Me

My journey into UX design begins with having a natural tendency to imagine more thoughtful innovations for everyday life experiences. My background is in project management and human resources. Throughout my time in the office I felt the need to discuss ways in which our processes or internal technology could improve in order to maintain a better work environment for everyone.

After realizing my favorite part about my role was gathering data, testing different work methods, and designing process flows and visuals for training materials, I decided to search for a career that would combine these with my personal interests in art, programming, and design. I found my calling within UX Design.

I love collaborating with a team, whether that’s at the workplace or on brainstorming weekends with my friends. Aside from designing I dabble in studio art, cover songs on my ukulele, and eat all the spicy food that New York City has to offer!
