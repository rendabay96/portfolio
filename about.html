About Me

How I Got Here

My journey into UX design begins with having a natural tendency to imagine more thoughtful innovations for everyday life experiences. Throughout my time working in project management and human resources, I loved discussing ways in which our processes or internal technology could improve in order to maintain a better work environment for everyone.

After realizing my favorite part about my role was gathering data, testing different work methods, and designing process flows and visuals for training materials, I decided to search for a career that would combine these with my personal interests in art, programming, and design. I found my calling in UX Design.

I love collaborating with a team, whether it’s at the workplace or on brainstorming weekends with my friends. Aside from designing I dabble in studio art, cover songs on my ukulele, and eat all the spicy food that New York City has to offer!
