Voyat

As an ecommerce optimization platform for hoteliers, Voyat helped hoteliers increase direct bookings by delivering targeted dynamic messages to visitors. Because we often worked with brand-conscious, luxury and boutique hotels, the Design Team helped rebrand all of our internal and externally-facing assets to consolidate our own brand identity.

Creative Brief

Voyat used predictive analytics to target hotel site visitors with relevant, natively designed messaging. By increasing direct bookings on hotel sites, we created lift in conversion rates. "Lift" became the theme for this rebranding excercise with which we put together the following creative brief.

Style Guide

The creative brief helped demonstrate the style that our externally-facing assets should embody. To reconcile the many assets that our various client-facing teams use, we created a style guide and branded everything over the course of a few weeks including pitch decks, our company portfolio, email templates, and performance reports.

Next Steps

Finally, we took the rebranding excercise one step further by creating a prototype for a Concierge App on which our clients can complete self-service onboarding to begin customizing their ad campaigns. Our hope was to set an example by building out this tool and any new products in the future with these newly defined brand guidelines to build credibility and trust with our clients.
