Hi, I'm Brenda.

My journey into UX design began with having a natural tendency to imagine more thoughtful innovations for everyday life experiences. When I realized my favorite parts about my pre-design roles were gathering data, testing different work methods, and designing process flows and visuals for training materials, I found my calling in user experience design. Today, I am a UX Designer at Intent Media, based in New York.
