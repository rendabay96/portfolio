About Brenda

I’m a UX Designer dedicated to addressing real-life problems with empathetic and elegant solutions. My background is in project management and human resources. After realizing my favorite part about my role was gathering data, testing different work methods, and designing process flows and visuals for training materials, I decided to search for a career that would combine these with my personal interests in art, programming, and design. I found my calling within UX Design.

Aside from designing I dabble in studio art, cover songs on my ukulele, and eat tons of spicy food!
