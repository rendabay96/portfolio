Website Design

Voyat

At Voyat, an ecommerce optimization platform for hoteliers, we as the design team were involved with the iterative process of discovering the brand of the company. Given that Voyat partnered primarily with luxury hotel brands, it was only appropriate for our own website to embody the same style. Provided with a new brand guideline from our marketing team, we revamped Voyatâ€™s homepage with a modern makeover.

Creating the Look

Originally, Voyat's homepage emphasized ease of implementation and predictive analytics, highlighting the technology behind the product. However, as the business focused more on providing better UX to our hoteliers' end users and delivering beautifully designed dynamic messaging that fit their brand, we felt that Voyat's own website needed a new look that appealed to the high-end, boutique/luxury clients we partnered with. Thus the site evolved from practical to personable.
