Creating The Look

Originally, Voyat's homepage emphasized ease of implementation and predictive analytics, highlighting the technology behind the product. However, as the business focused more on providing better UX to our hoteliers' end users and delivering beautifully designed dynamic messaging that fit their brand, we felt that Voyat's own website needed a new look that appealed to the high-end, boutique/luxury clients we partnered with.
